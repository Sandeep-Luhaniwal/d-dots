How It Works

At D.Dots, we employ fully homomorphic encryption (FHE) to revolutionize the confidentialityof your data. Unlike traditional encryption that must be decrypted for any computation, ouradvanced FHE methods ensure that your data remains encrypted during the entire analyticalprocess, mitigating the risk of exposure and maintaining privacy integrity.
